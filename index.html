WHY PEOPLE KEEP COMING BACK

We're not trying to be fancy.
We just make food that feels like home.

This is a family place. We open early, we stay late enough, and we make sure your plate looks like someone actually cares you’re eating. The portions are big because that’s how we were raised — you don’t leave hungry. The prices are fair because we know what it’s like to work for a living.

Whether it’s eggs the way you like them at 8am, a hot bowl of soup on a cold day, or Friday fish the way it should be, we’re here for the regulars and the first-timers alike. Come in, sit wherever you like, and let us take care of you.

THE FAMILY BEHIND THE PLATE

It started with a 13-year-old dishwasher.

Frank Bobola fell in love with the restaurant business as a kid and never looked back — from line cook to chef to opening his own neighborhood spots. In 2018 the family carried his name and his way of doing things to 9 Simon Street.

His rule was simple: treat people right, give them more than they expect, and keep the food honest. That's still exactly how we run the place.

Frank Bobola
