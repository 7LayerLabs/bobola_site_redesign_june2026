WHY PEOPLE KEEP COMING BACK

We're not trying to be fancy.
We just make food that feels like home.

This is a family place. We open early, we make sure your plate looks like someone actually cares you're eating, and the portions are big because that's how we were raised, you don't leave hungry. The prices are fair because we know what it's like to work for a living.

Whether it's eggs the way you like them in the morning, a hot bowl of soup on a cold day, or a Friday dinner that hits the spot, we're here for the regulars and the first-timers alike. Come in, sit wherever you like, and let us take care of you.

OUR STORY

Honoring where we came from, and where we're headed.

Bobola's started as a tribute to our father, Frank, the cook who taught us that good food and good people belong together. Eight years in, his spirit still runs this kitchen.

But this next chapter isn't only about looking back. It's about the food we put out today, the family running the place now, and the neighbors who make it worth doing. We're keeping his legacy alive the best way we know how, by moving it forward.
